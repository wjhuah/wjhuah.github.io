Hello!

I am Wenjie Hua, a junior student at Wuhan University with an exchange at University of Notre Dame, focusing on paleography, linguistics, and history. Previously, I served as an editorial assistant on science and humanities at Science Press. Academically, I worked on code-switching at Eberhard Language Lab, did research on legal history and politics, advised by Dr. Liang Cai, and led projects on computer vision for ancient printings, supervised by Dr. Yinzong Wei at WHU Ancient Books Institute.

Beyond academics, I served as vice president of the Chunying 萅英 Poetry Club, and my poems were published in several literary journals. Also, I am a loyal outdoor enthusiast, mountain climbing and cave exploring are my favorites.
